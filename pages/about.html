ABOUT US



Nohea (noh-HEH-AH) Hawaiian word which means "Beautiful". Resort wear that is known to be sophisticated and sultry. At Nohea Style, we make sure that you are wearing comfort and style.

The collections inspire the feeling of tropical vibes, beach holidays and can be worn just as a stroll on the beach or even a girls' day out in the city. The colors and patterns of each Nohea Style piece have been inspired by the founders' personal travels and experience.

Quality is our utmost priority. We ensure our pieces will give you comfort and style exactly as they need to. We work with Filipino experts in this field to maintain high quality we expect ourselves, and you have come to expect of us. Wash with care, you can find the care instruction inside every piece.

Based in the Philippines, Nohea Style is currently available on Instagram and Facebook @noheastyle, we deliver nationwide. For international shipping, you can send us an email at info@noheastyle.com.
